Legal & Privacy

Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

1. Zero Data Retention Policy

The Phishing Detector prioritizes your privacy. We operate on a strict Zero Data Retention model regarding your scanned content.

  • No Storage: We do not log, store, database, or archive the email bodies, sender addresses, or URLs you submit for scanning.
  • Ephemeral Processing: Data is processed in server memory (RAM) solely for the duration of the scan and is instantly discarded once the risk score is generated.
  • Analytics: We may record anonymous, aggregated metadata (such as the total number of safe vs. unsafe scans performed globally) to display system analytics, but this data cannot be tied back to any specific user or specific email content.

2. Acceptable Use & Unauthorized Access

This service is provided for educational and defensive security purposes. Any attempt to abuse, exploit, or disrupt this infrastructure is strictly prohibited.

The following actions will be considered hostile and may be reported to law enforcement:

  • Attempting to bypass security controls, reverse-engineer the machine learning models, or execute code injections (including XSS or SQLi).
  • Performing Denial of Service (DoS/DDoS) attacks or deliberately flooding the scanner with automated, high-volume API requests.
  • Using the platform to validate or refine the effectiveness of your own malicious phishing campaigns.

Violations of these terms are subject to prosecution under applicable cyber laws, including the Information Technology Act, 2000.
